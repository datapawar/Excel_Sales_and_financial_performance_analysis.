# Sales-financial-performance-analysis-using-Excel.
These files provide detailed sales, market, and financial analytics for AtliQ Hardwares from 2019-2021. They include customer and product performance, market vs target comparisons, division and India-specific reports, quarterly gross margins, and profit &amp; loss statements by market, all in USD, supporting strategic insights.
## Sales Report :


- **Project objective:** 

 **1.** Create a _[customer performance report](https://github.com/Abhishek-Pawar08/Sales-financial-performance-analysis-using-Excel./blob/main/1.%20customer%20performance%20report.pdf)_ 

 **2.** Generate focused _[India-specific sales analytics](https://github.com/Abhishek-Pawar08/Sales-financial-performance-analysis-using-Excel./blob/main/2.%20India%20specific.pdf)_

 **3.** Conduct a comprehensive comparison between _[market performance and sales targets](https://github.com/Abhishek-Pawar08/Sales-financial-performance-analysis-using-Excel./blob/main/3.%20market%20performance%20vs%20target.pdf)_

- **Purpose of sales analytics:** Empower businesses to monitor and evaluate their sales activities and performance.

- **Importance of analyzing sales data:** Identify sales patterns and track key performance indicators (KPIs).

- **Role of reports:** Determine effective customer discounts, facilitate negotiations with consumers, and identify potential business expansion opportunities in promising countries.
